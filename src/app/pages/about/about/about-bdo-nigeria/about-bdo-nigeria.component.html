BDO & CO.(BDO) is a professional services firm registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The firm is led by a team of knowledge-driven experts specializing in diverse business fields. Our mission is to empower businesses to grow, thrive, and achieve a substantial and lasting financial impact by delivering value-added and strategic business development solutions.

We provide tailored, high-quality support services, advisory, and insights for various types of businesses, from for-profits to non-profits and startups. Our focus is on ensuring clients' profitability and growth through innovative strategies that drive efficiency and enhance competitiveness.

At BDO & CO. we work with organizations of all sizes: micro, small, medium, and large enterprises. We are committed to being by your side every step of the way, helping you and your business achieve your goals and aims. Our value-adding services cover key areas such as Advisory, Audit & Assurance, Tax.
